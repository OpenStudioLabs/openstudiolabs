Open-Source Engineering Residency Program

Accelerate Your Career in Open-Source Engineering

Open Studio Labs offers a "first-of-its-kind" residency program designed for future engineers looking to make a real impact on critical, upstream open-source (OS) projects. Modeled after medical residencies, this 4 to 6-year program provides an OS resident with hands-on experience, contributing to under-supported open-source projects while gaining valuable expertise in security, code quality, and project sustainability.

Why Join?

  • Real-World Impact: Work on essential open-source projects used by commercial enterprises.
  • Learn from Experts: Unite with leading open-source contributors and maintainers.
  • Public Portfolio: Share your open-source contributions and showcase your achievements.

Call to Action

How It Works

  • See It: Learn by observing experienced engineers working on real-world open-source issues.
  • Learn It: Gain hands-on experience in addressing security vulnerabilities, technical debt, and project maintenance.
  • Teach It: Sharing and contributing to the open-source community the knowledge you gained.

Are you Ready?

Ready to learn what it takes to be an OS engineer and shape the future of open-source at Apache, Linux, or Rust Foundation? Sign up today to learn about the Open Studio Labs Residency Program and craft your legacy in the open-source community.
