About Us

We understand the unique challenges companies face when they utilize Open Source (OS) Software.

Open Studio Labs is a pioneering research and development lab dedicated to solving the critical challenge of open source (OS) software talent shortages. We stand out with our unique Engineering Residency (ER) model, the "first-of-its-kind." This model equips highly skilled U.S.-based engineers to contribute to crucial open source ecosystems, helping enterprises tackle mounting technical debt, mitigate security vulnerabilities, and support under-supported OS projects.

Our Mission is to support enterprises by developing a sustainable talent pipeline of skilled people proficient in upstream OS work. This allows companies to focus on innovation and customer needs, confident they are not hindered by technical debt challenges.

Our Approach Inspired by the medical residency model, our residences follow a "See It, Learn It, Teach It" methodology, delivering hands-on solutions for the growing complexities in OS software. We aim to close the void between talent famine and the ever-increasing need for secure, efficient, OS projects that companies trust.
