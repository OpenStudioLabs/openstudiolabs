About Us

We understand the unique challenges companies face
when they utilize Open-Source Software.

Too often, businesses have become accustomed to tackling the tasks of getting new features, fixing bug issues, and enhancing cybersecurity through workarounds or pestering the OS Maintainer.

Our solution is a game-changer for businesses, empowering them to operate confidently and efficiently by reducing technical debt through engineering support to under-resourced OS projects.

Open Studio Labs is progressing with the Development-as-a-Service (DaaS) platform, a solution that provides continuous development and support for open-source software. Our Residency offers real-world apprenticeship and mentorship to our OS engineer contributors, helping them enhance and secure the open-source software they depend upon.

In our commitment to being a key player in the OSS ecosystem, we create upstream Enterprise Open Source Software for a Better World. When businesses look back at our contribution, it's not just significant; it's transformative, and we're proud to be part of this inspiring journey.

Our Engineering Residency nurtures the next generation of engineers who contribute to the open-source relied upon by most technology companies.
