About Us

<

We understand the unique challenges companies face
when they utilize Open-Source Software.

Open Studio Labs is a pioneering research and development lab dedicated to solving the critical challenge of open source software talent shortages. We stand out with our unique residency model, the "first-of-its-kind." This model equips highly skilled U.S.-based engineers to contribute to crucial open source ecosystems, helping enterprises tackle mounting technical debt, mitigate security vulnerabilities, and support under-supported open source projects.

Our Mission is to support enterprises by developing a sustainable talent pipeline of skilled engineers proficient in upstream open source work. This allows companies to focus on innovation and customer needs, confident they are not hindered by technical debt challenges.

Our Approach Inspired by the medical residency model, our engineers follow a "See It, Learn It, Teach It" methodology, delivering hands-on solutions for the growing complexities in open source software. We aim to close the void between talent famine and the ever-increasing need for secure, efficient, open source projects that companies trust.

What We Do
